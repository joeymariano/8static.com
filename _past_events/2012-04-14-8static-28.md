description: |-
  This month, 8static's lineup is going to seriously blow some minds. For our
  second show at PhilaMOCA we've got some serious talent for you all. If you've
  seen him playing at the open mics lately you'll know that Disassembler brings
  serious bass, and danceable grooves. He'll be treating your ears to his unique
  brand of Nanoloop beats. Also playing this month is Baltimore's Shawnphase , who
  takes a different view on things with real rocking jams. With a metal influence
  and chip medium his awesome tunes are not something to be missed. Third up is a
  legend both in 8static history and in chip music. All the way from the west
  coast, after having seen Tokyo and Melbourne, Trash80 is coming to Philly. His
  last performance in Philadelphia, back in November of 2009, is still spoken of
  in hushed voices as one of the greatest performances that 8static has ever seen.
  And keeping your vision entranced, Visualicious will be providing her unique
  digital stylings. If you've seen her visuals you know it's unlike any other!
  This is not a show you want to miss! Before the show gets started, at 7pm we'll
  be having a workshop with Dino Lionetti , of Cheap Dinosaurs, who will be
  talking about how to write chip music with odd time signatures. The Open mic
  follows immediately, with limited spots, so come early to sign up! Share and
