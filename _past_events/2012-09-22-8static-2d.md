description: |-
  On September 22nd we celebrate the triumphant return of Buffalo's guitar
  shredding maestro, Danimal Cannon . Since blowing our minds here one year ago,
  Danimal has brought his guitar / game boy combo up & down the east coast on tour
  with rock-opera super-group, the Protomen and rocked huge events such as Blip
  Festival, MAGFest & PAX East. It's an honor to welcome him back to
  Philadelphia!! On the other side of the Mason-Dixon line we welcome the 8static
  debut of LSDJ wizard Trey Frey . Trey's infectious dance grooves & high energy
  performance has earned him a dedicated fan base in West Virginia & Maryland. His
  complex & densely-layered jams will surely win over anyone who has yet to
  experience him live. Oven Rake is joining us from across the country, all the
  way from Tacoma, WA. Best known for his funky gameboy electronica, last month
  saw the debut of his entry into post-rock with the Boy's Club project. We can't
  wait to hear what he will bring to 8static! Philadelphia's own Animal Style
  twisted our minds with his special blend of organic / digital art for visuals at
  his last 8static. He has been working on totally new material that he will debut
  this show! The real-time rendering of game engines opens up possibilities for
  audiovisual performance. Game art duo Foci + Loci will present an examination of
  their work process in Little Big Planet 2 providing an opportunity for
  participants to collectively build and perform in a simulated environment. Join
  us at 7pm for our workshop hosted by Chris Burke (Glomag) and Tamara Yadao
  (Corset Lore) and experience a new way to combine music, art &#038; video
  gaming.
