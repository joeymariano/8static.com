  Poster by animal style We celebrate our 4th anniversary at 8static on October
  13th! This will be a very special, all-star event that you won't want to miss at
  any cost! Back in 2008, the legendary Bit Shifter played the very first 8static
  at Studio 34. We are very honored to bring him back for our anniversary this
  month (as he also celebrates his birthday) and blow our minds with amazing LSDJ
  dance grooves. Coming off his iconic Blip Festival performance this year, Radlib
  makes his 8static debut and will close out the night with some new material for
  us. Staging a retro-dance party with dual vintage FM synthesis enabled laptops,

  Philadelphia showcase artist this year is the amazing an0va . A little math-rock

  took the community by storm last year with his debut release, "The Teaching
  Machine". We are very proud to have him representing phillychip! Last, but not
  least we have some freshly emerging talent from Michigan, Nikola Whallon .

  our show this month. Our visualists all come from Philly this month. 8static

  will beautify all the fantastic music through our new double-projector setup.

  8static. Presenting 12 veteran performers from 8statics past, we will debut our

  this. DO NOT MISS! IF you can't make it, be sure to check out our live audio
