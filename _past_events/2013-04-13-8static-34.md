  Br1ght Pr1mate • Note! • Dauragon • Environmental Sound Collapse April 2nd, 2013
  by emfed Poster design by Julie Elizabeth Spring is here, time to leave your
  homes & come to the next 8static on April 13th at PhilaMOCA ! We've got another
  fantastic show of national acts planned for you this month!! First we have the
  legendary Br1ght Pr1mate returning from their winter hibernation in Boston.
  After playing such big events as MAGFest 11 and PAX East, it will great to
  welcome back to a more intimate environment at 8static! The duo, composed of
  intricate LSDJ dance jams with soulful female vocals, are potential future
  superstars in the scene. Having crafted a special Star Trek-themed set for our
  Tuesday Tune-out series (complete with costumes and new songs!), they will be
  bringing all that hard work to show at 8static proper! Animal Style will be
  sharing his custom visuals for their set. It's been over a year since Note! 's
  last show at 8static and he will be coming back this month with all new material
  for his upcoming album release (possibly available at the show!). Note!'s
  infectious & complex dance grooves will keep all of us on our feet during his
  set. We really look forward to having him back since our move to PhilaMOCA. One
  of our most original sounding musicians is coming up from Washington, D.C.
  Dauragon has mastered the art of super-bass on the Gameboy with nanoloop. Deep,
  heavy beats with compelling grooves are just the start to his performance as his
  dark & somber melodies flow in you will be mesmerized by his sonic curtain.
  Definitely not something you wanna miss if you've never experienced his set
  before! Joining us all the way from Chicago we are treated to a rare opportunity
  to experience a live set by Environmental Sound Collapse . E.S.C. will challenge
  your senses with dark, experimental compositions that are very uncommon lately
  in the chip music scene. E.S.C. will also be providing us visuals for the night.
  We welcome our longtime friend to his 8static debut and can't wait to see what
  he brings for us!! Our workshop is back this month with Animal Style giving us
  some pro-tips on how to solder. This is an ideal place to learn if you've been
  wanting to tackle some circuit-bending or mod your own equipment, so don't miss
  it! If that isn't enough for you, Radlib is back at our after-party! Join us
  upstairs at The Institute Bar down the block and hear him DJ some 80&#8242;s
  tunes!! Also don't forget, as an 8static patron your bill will be 10% off!
  Admission will be a sliding scale of $7-$10. Those that support 8static with a
  $10 admission fee will receive a special sticker exclusive to this month's show!
  Spread the word and get your friends to join us at another amazing 8static at
  PhilaMOCA !!! RSVP at our Facebook event: Invite your friends here!!
