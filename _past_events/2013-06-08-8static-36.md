description: |-
  Elizabeth 8static is this Saturday June 8th! To start the party, NO CARRIER, one
  of the founders of 8static, will continue his workshop series on live visuals
  and game hardware. A veteran Philadelphia hacker and visualist currently
  residing in New York, NO CARRIER will teach us how to create visuals for the
  Nintendo Entertainment System! The workshop follows with our 60 minute open mic,
  and then our headliners: First Act: Storm Blooper started as a regular at our
  open-mics, started playing small shows around Philadelphia, and now rightfully
  takes the opener 8static slot! All grown up? Don’t count on it. Storm Blooper
  keeps his sense of humor. Direct, fun, and batteries fully charged, these are
  Gameboy tracks to keep you dancing! Second Act: The high-speed chip pop of
  Kentucky! Kentucky? Damn straight! Solarbear writes for Gameboy and what comes
  out is fast, melodic, and caffeinated. Get ready to spill some Kentucky all over
  the stage and into the crowd. This act is a lot of fun to watch! Final Act:
  Hailing from Paris, France, we are excited to present the headliner Ultrasyd!
  Gaining momentum after a European tour and a veteran of Blipfestival 2011,
  Ultrasyd writes insistent and hypnotic music with a demoscene-meets-house sound.
  Get ready for some french house music through the Atari ST, Gameboy, and other
  vintage computers! After party: After the last act, we continue the party one
  block down at the Institute. The legendary DJ Cutman will be spinning the best
  video game remixes ever!!!
