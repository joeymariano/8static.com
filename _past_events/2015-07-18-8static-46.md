description: |-
  8static, Philly’s premier 8-bit/chiptune audio/visual party, is back at a new
  venue and ALL AGES again! We have a killer lineup for this benefit show to raise
  money to improve our events and raise festival awareness. Famitracker
  Worksop/Open Mic In the tradition of 8static's past, we will kick things off
  with an informative workshop by ap0c on composing with famitracker at 7pm,
  followed by open mic at 8pm (five minutes/one song max.), and dive right into
  the show at 9pm. Don’t miss it! Visual Performance SBthree ( Rochester, NY )
  Using a mix of extremely simple 3-D objects and abstract pixel art, Steven Boyar
  ( SBthree ) creates looping animations and strings them together to music. A
  handful of glitchy video effects are added for good measure. It's anyone's guess
  how the scientist plans to hypnotize us. Performers Danimal Cannon ( Buffalo, NY
  ) Guitarist, chiptuner, producer, engineer, MUSICIAN. You might know this
  Buffalo, NY, native from work with Armcannon , Metroid Metal , or Weaponex or
  his numerous PAX and MAGfest appearances, his acclaimed 2011 album "Roots," or
  "Parallel Processing," his collaboration with Zef. But until you catch his
  impossible grooves and clever hooks, unmistakable guitar solos and intricate
  LSDJ arrangements live, you don't know Danimal Cannon . TREY FREY ( PHL )
  Straight outta the WV, and recently relocated to Philadelphia, Trey Frey creates
  music using two synced 1989 gameboys. His 2014 album 'Refresh' quickly cemented
  him at the forefront of the chip music scene with soaring, catchy melodies,
  intricate sound design, and a respect and understanding for dance music.
  Solarbear ( PHL ) Another Philly transplant moving in all the way from
  Lexington, Kentucky. Solarbear makes progressive metal chiptunes with a bit of
  that good feelin' thrown in. If you're ready to dance&#8230; step aside and let
  the moshers in.
